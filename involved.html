GET INVOLVED

Our Collective+ sessions are open to absolutely anyone with an interest in singing. For keen amateur singers these workshops offer an exciting opportunity to sing alongside professional singers from the worlds of opera and musical theatre. Previous Collective+ workshops have included an a capella singing day with the wonderful Jennifer John (vocal coach on ITV’s The Voice) and a Musical Theatre singing day with Joe Bunker, Ben Cox and Adey Grummet.
